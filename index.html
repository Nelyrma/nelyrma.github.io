About

An image compressor to optimize the size of your image files without compromising quality.

The title highlights the project name and its main functionality.
The goal is to provide a quick and efficient solution to reduce the size of images which will save time and storage space for users who need to share or store large images.

As a novice developer, I embarked on my first solo project which is also my portfolio project for Holberton School, ShrinkPic. Every day, I explored new technologies and learned new concepts to bring my project to fruition. Despite the challenges and moments of frustration, I stuck with it and progressed thanks to my determination.

I chose to do an image compression project for several reasons. Firstly, image size can have a significant impact on website performance. Secondly, image compression saves storage space and reduces loading times. Images are a powerful means of communication and expression in our technology-driven generation. They are essential for conveying messages, creating engaging experiences and, above all, images play an important role in preserving memories, as they capture moments, emotions and stories that remain etched in our memories and can be passed down through time which happens to be the real reason I chose to do this project.

Features

ShrinkPic's main function is to reduce image size.
It allows the user to choose an image to compress from his file manager with the "Select the image" button, to delete the image if it's not the right image he wanted with the "Delete" button and then to generate the compressed image right next to the original image using the "Generate" button to compare the two images side by side, and finally to save the compressed image with the "Download" button.
